📦 E-Commerce Order & Shipping - FAQs

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email and SMS containing a tracking number and a tracking link.

Shipping charges depend on your delivery location and order value.

  • Free shipping on orders above ₹999
  • Standard shipping fee of ₹49 for orders below ₹999
  • Express delivery charges may apply for selected locations

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery.

We accept multiple secure payment options, including:

  • Credit Cards & Debit Cards
  • Net Banking
  • UPI
  • Cash on Delivery
